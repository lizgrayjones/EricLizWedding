Traveling?

We’re so excited that our friends & family are traveling to our little town to share this day with us. Here’s how to get here and where to stay.

Flights

The Philadelphia Airport (PHL) will be your cheapest bet with the most flight options. Allentown Airport (ABE) is another option, usually more costly. Both are about an hour’s drive away from the wedding location.

Where To Stay

Or, Pitch A Tent

Ideal for the budget-conscious or anyone who wants to party ‘til the sun comes up: Bring your own tent and camp out right in the backyard. If it rains, you can still pitch your tent under the big tent, space permitting. If you plan to camp please let us know!

Getting Around

A rental car will be your best bet – Ubers and taxis are scarce in our neck of the woods.

Parking At The House

When you get onto Cepp Road, look for signs showing you where to park. If the driveway at 20 Cepp Road is full, you may have to park in a neighbor’s driveway. If so, signs will be there to show you where to go. Parking may be a little crazy, so please bear with us we get it all figured out!
